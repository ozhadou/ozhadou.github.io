## About OzHadou
OzHadou was first created in August 2001 by Ziggy (Andrew) and Final Atomic Buster (Justin) to help promote the Australian Street Fighter scene via online discussion, casual play and regular tournaments. It also allowed local scenes from all over the country to connect with each other.

Our current focus is on offline Sydney events where communities across all fighting games can gather together. These events are targeted for all fighting game fans, whether you are casual, competitive or somewhere in between. The only requirement is to enjoy fighting games!

Currently we run two events:
* **York Street Battles**: our monthly event hosted in Club York in the Sydney CBD
* **OzHadou Nationals**: our annual major tournament which attracts both national and international attendees and viewership
